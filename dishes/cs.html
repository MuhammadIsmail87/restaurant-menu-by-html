Chicken Steak

Chicken steak is a versatile and flavorful dish that transforms a simple chicken breast or thigh into a hearty meal. Typically, the chicken is pounded or sliced to an even thickness, seasoned with a blend of herbs and spices, and then grilled, pan-seared, or baked to achieve a golden-brown exterior and tender, juicy interior. The dish often features a delicious sauce or marinade, such as a creamy mushroom sauce, tangy barbecue glaze, or a zesty lemon-herb dressing, which enhances the natural flavor of the chicken. Accompanied by sides like roasted vegetables, fluffy mashed potatoes, or a crisp salad, chicken steak provides a satisfying alternative to traditional beef steaks. Its adaptability and lighter profile make it a popular choice for those seeking a healthier yet equally delicious meal.
